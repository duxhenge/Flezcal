Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Tap any action that requires an account (like adding a spot or leaving a review) and you'll be prompted to sign in. You can use Sign in with Apple or create an account with your email address.

How do I add a new spot?

Tap the ghost pin on the map or the "Add Spot" button in the list view. Search for the venue, confirm the details, and submit. Our system will verify the listing before it appears for other users.

How do I change my display name?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Change Display Name." Your display name is shown publicly with your reviews and on the leaderboard.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account, reviews, and associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Why does the app need my location?

Flezcal uses your location to show nearby spots on the map and sort results by distance. Location data is used only within the app and is not stored on our servers. You can revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings.

How do I report inappropriate content?

On any review, tap the flag icon to report it. Our team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action.
